Cybernation is a tech fest and envisions providing a platform for faculty members, research scholars, students, and
industry experts who are passionate about cybersecurity to unite and share their innovative ideas while learning about
how technology can be integrated with myriad other fields. It is intended to provide collaboration initiatives among
universities and industries. Amrita CyberNation will be a platform for initiating discussions on challenging aspects of
Cyber Security for our Nation. Students will also get an opportunity to interact and participate in many challenges in
Cyber Domain where participants are from throughout the nation.

The event will be inaugurated by Dr. G. Athithan, Former Director General and Distinguished Scientist, and currently DRDO Chair,
CAIR, Bengaluru. His research interests include Information Security Management, Computational Intelligence, Network Data Mining,
and Cyber Security Technologies.
